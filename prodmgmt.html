Product Management

All images are reconstructions and do not contain actual client data

As a product manager it’s my job to make sure everyone on the team has a clear understanding of our client and the problems they are facing. I make sure that whatever we are building is going to help solve a need, and that we are building the features that will have the biggest impact.

Performance Management Dashboards

It used to be that everyone in the organization put together their own report to get at these metrics. There was a lot of frustration with inconsistent methods and numbers randomly changing. The need was to create something where users could access their data on demand, and have it be one version of the truth that everyone could rely on.

This development involved merging data from many different subsystems; pulling from API’s, databases, and even text scraping. With all of the data merged we had to create rules for normalizing the data, and transforming it into a schema that microstrategy could efficiently work with.

Clerk Metrics Mart

Throughout the process of developing the performance dashboards it became apparent that not all data collection can be 100% automated. Maybe its qualitative, or maybe there are political issues preventing integration. Rather than passing around files, consolidating, and loading into the database we created a web form entry system. It allows stakeholders to enter certain data points about their areas each month.
